Peter Lustiber

Biography:

Peter Michael Lustiber (born April 25, 1955) is a business consultant specializing in market research and technology for the Consumer Packaged Goods industry. He was born in Watertown, MA (a suburb of Boston) to George Lustiber and Margret Lustiber (née McCoy). He was the middle of 5 children and the eldest son. Since childhood, Peter has been an ardent Boston sports fan and has followed their "progress" over the years regardless of where he has lived.

Peter graduated from Harvard College where he majored in Engineering and Applied Physics. While at Harvard, he met his wife Laurie Lustiber (née Ferguson) who was also a student. They have a son Gregory who recently graduated from Rice University and resides in Houston, TX.

Peter and Laurie currently live in Wilmington, NC where he is retired and she works for the local hospital. Peter still loves his Red Sox and they both enjoy reading, doing crossword puzzles, going to trivia contests and taking walks on the beach.
